Detailed Itinerary

Day 01 - Airport - Kandy

Travel Time: Approx 3 hours
First off, welcome to Sri Lanka.
On arrival at the airport, you will be met by our National Tour Guide who will heartily greet you and transfer you from the Airport directly to Kandy.
Kandy is well renowned for the Temple of the Tooth and the Royal Botanical Gardens. Encircled by hills, with a tranquil lake in the center, Kandy is a city with a cool climate. It is a cultural sanctuary where many legends, traditions and folklore are still kept alive, Kandy and its satellite villages are the centre of the islands handicraft industry, exquisite silver or gold jewellery and precious gems of many varieties including the world’s best blue and star sapphires.
In Kandy, we will first check in to the hotel and unwind. You can relax by the poolside or opt for any activity you prefer engaging in. After dinner you will rest well and prepare for the beginning of your Sri Lankan adventure.

Day 02 - Kandy - Pinnawela - Kandy

Travel Time: Approx 45 minutes
After breakfast at the hotel, you will visit the Pinnawela Elephant Orphanage. The Elephant Orphanage is a major tourist attraction where you will see herds of abandoned elephants taken care off with tender love and protection. This 24 acres large orphanage is also a breeding place for elephants, more than twenty elephants were born since 1984, and it has the greatest herd of elephants in captivity in the world.
After spending some memorable time with the elephants, you will return to Kandy and indulge in a city tour and visit a popular gem mining factory and a popular marketplace to get the feel of real Sri Lanka. Thereafter we will engage in a Cultural Show and later visit the famous Temple of the tooth which is also popularly known as the “Dalada Maligawa”.
Afterwards, you will return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay in Kandy.




Day 03 - Kandy - Nuwara Eliya

Travel Time: Approx 2 hours
You will leave Kandy after breakfast en route to Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka's premier hill resort with a brilliantly refreshing mountain climate. Nuwara Eliya is also the heart of Sri Lanka's tea country, producing some of the world's best tea. Mountain slopes and valleys are carpeted with lush green tea plantations, combined with gushing streams and tumbling waterfalls making it a mountain paradise. An 18-hole golf course and a race course add to the scenic beauty.
You will spend your day in Nuwara Eliya visiting places of interest and taking a walk in the picturesque city. Thereafter, you will return back to the hotel for dinner a good night's sleep.

Day 04 - Nuwara Eliya - Horton Plains - Nuwara Eliya

Travel Time: Approx 1 hour (One way to the entrance)
After a hearty breakfast to fuel yourself up, we embark on a more physically challenging adventure - a trek to the picturesque Horton Plains. The trek lasts approximately 3 hours.
Horton Plains is Sri Lanka's highest plateau - 7,200 feet above sea level in the central mountain country. It is an extremely scenic region with interesting wildlife, astounding mountains, grassy valleys, streams and marshes. You can visit the 'World's End' where the plateau plunges a thousand feet in a straight sheer drop or relax by a picturesque waterfall.
After trekking and exploring Horton Plains, we return back to the hotel for some well earned relaxation. You can enjoy the hill countryside with a walk around peaceful Nuwara Eliya in the evening and visiting places of interest. Thereafter, you will return back to the hotel for dinner and a good night's sleep.

Day 05 - Nanu Oya (By Train) - Bandarawela - Adhisham Bungalow

Travel Time: Approx 1 hour
After breakfast you will check out from the hotel and be transferred to Nanu Oya railway station for a short train journey to Bandarawela.
Bandarawela is a peaceful hill country town with a colonial flavor. This can be seen in the many old buildings which comprise the city. You will check in at the Bandarawela Hotel and enjoy the cool climate. We will visit the Adhisham Bungalow in Haputale, which is an old Benedictine monastery built in the shape of a small castle. It's architecture is beautiful and is a sight to see. You can also purchase fresh fruit jams / preservatives and juices prepared by the monks from the fruit grown in their gardens.
We go back to Bandarawela Hotel to spend the evening and dinner.

Day 06 - Bandarawela - Kitulgala - Colombo or Uswetakeiyawa

Travel Time: Approx 2.5 hours
You will leave Bandarawela after breakfast at the hotel and proceed to Colombo or Uswetakeiyawa, a hotel close to the Airport, via visiting Kitulgala on the way.
Previously known for being the location for the film 'Bridge On The River Kwai', Kitulgala is fast gaining popularity for the thrilling white water rafting.White water rafting on the Kelani river from Kitulgala is one of the most exciting and accessible activities Sri Lanka has to offer. The area and the scenery around Kitulgala is stunning, tucked away in the midst of ravines in the picturesque hill country.
Thereafter we will transfer you to Colombo (Deluxe Program) OR to a hotel close to the Airport in Uswetakeiyawa (Standard Programme).
Dinner and overnight stay in the program location you choose.

Day 07 - Colombo or Uswetakeiyawa - Departure

Travel Time: Approx 3 hours
Breakfast and checkout from the hotel on time before you leave to the airport in time for departure, soaking up the memories and experiences of the last 4 days in our paradise island. Your memorable journey with Ceylon Roots comes to an end. (Of course, you are welcome to extend your stay, to maybe visit the beaches in Sri Lanka or any other program you may enjoy. Just let us know in advance.)
We bid you farewell at the airport and wish you a safe flight back home, hoping you would come back again and visit the parts of Sri Lanka that you haven't experienced yet.
