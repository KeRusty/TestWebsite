Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Airport - Galle

Travel Time: Approx 2 hours
First off, welcome to Sri Lanka.
On arrival at the airport, you will be met by our National Tour Guide who will heartily greet you and transfer you from the Airport directly to Galle (Standard Program).
Galle is a beautiful vibrant location filled with culture and beautiful beaches as well as the women.
You can enjoy the evening at the hotel. Overnight stay and dinner will be at the hotel in Galle.

Day 02 - Galle - Mirissa

Travel Time: Approx 1.5 hours

After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, we will move to Mirissa, which has some of the most bautiful beaches in the world.
After lunch we will take a jungle excursion on a Jeep Safari Around Mirissa.
After the safari you will return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay,


Day 03 - Mirissa - Arugam Bay

Travel Time: Approx 4 hour
After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, we will move to Arugam Bay, The Surf Capital of the Indian Ocean.
After lunch, and a break to rejuvenate you, we will move on to visit the small dinghy fishing town of Pottuvil which is centered in the heart of Arugam Bay
After visiting Pottuvil and the perusing the beaches nearby, you will return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay,

Day04 - Arugam Bay - Departure

Travel Time: Approx 7 hour
After breakfast, You will leave Arugam Bay after breakfast en route to the airport, soaking up the memories and experiences of the last 4 days in our paradise island. Your memorable journey with Ceylon Roots comes to an end. (Of course, you are welcome to extend your stay, to maybe visit the beaches in Sri Lanka or any other program you may enjoy. Just let us know in advance.)
We bid you farewell at the airport and wish you a safe flight back home, hoping you would come back again and visit the parts of Sri Lanka that you haven't experienced yet.
