Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Airport - Dambulla or Habarana - Optional Elephant Back Safari

Travel Time: Approx 3.5 hours
First off, welcome to Sri Lanka.
On arrival at the airport, you will be met by our National Tour Guide who will heartily greet you and transfer you from the Airport directly to Dambulla (Standard Program) or Habarana (Deluxe Program).
Dambulla is mostly famous for the rock temple consisting of five cave temples constructed in the 1st Century BC. Dambulla is home to many lakes and reservoirs built by the kings of ancient times. Apart from the historic sites in Dambulla and Habarana, be prepared to witness breathtakingly beautiful landscape and fertile habitat.
You can enjoy the evening at the hotel or experience an Elephant back Safari through the jungles of Habarana. Overnight stay and dinner will be at the hotel in Dambulla or Habarana.

Day 02 - Dambulla or Habarana - Anuradhapura - Optional Jeep Safari

Travel Time: Approx 1.5 hours

After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, we will move to Polonnaruwa, the second medieval capital of Sri Lanka. Built during the 11th and 12th Centuries AD, it is one of the best preserved cities in Sri Lanka. interesting sites to visit are the Royal Palace, the spectacular Gal Viharaya complex where wonderful statues of Lord Buddha in different postures can be seen..
After lunch we will take a jungle excursion on a Jeep Safari to Minneriya National park to witness the wild elephants. Best time to visit is from June to September. During this time it is possible to see herds of up to 150 elephants feeding and washing, as well as, sambar deer and leopards. The hungry bird flocks include cormorants and painted storks.
After the safari you will return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay,


Day 03 - Dambulla or Habarana - Polonnaruwa - Sigiriya

Travel Time: Approx 1 hour
After a healthy breakfast at the hotel, we will move to Polonnaruwa, the second medieval capital of Sri Lanka. Built during the 11th and 12th Centuries AD, it is one of the best preserved cities in Sri Lanka. interesting sites to visit are the Royal Palace, the spectacular Gal Viharaya complex where wonderful statues of Lord Buddha in different postures can be seen.
After lunch, and a break to rejuvenate you, we will move on to visit Sigiriya, one of Sri Lanka's biggest attractions. Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a spectacular rock fortress built by King Kashyapa. Be entranced by the Water Gardens, the Sigiri Frescoes, the Mirror Wall of ancient grafitti, the Lion platform and the main Summit on top of the rock.
After visiting Sigiriya, you will return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay,

Day04 - Dambulla or Habarana - Matale Spice Garden - Kandy

Travel Time: Approx 1 hour
After breakfast, we travel to the town of Kandy, after visiting the aforementioned Dambulla rock temples.
Dambulla rock temples consists of five cave temples constructed in the 1st Century BC. Today the temple is a World Heritage Site which contains many statues and paintings dating back to the 12th century and some of the latest ones of the 18th Century.
Thereafter, we proceed to Kandy while visiting the Matale Spice Garden on the way. The Spice Garden has an assortment of luscious gardens containing Cinnamon, Cardamoms, Pepper plants and many more spices for which Sri Lanka is famous for. See how these spices are grown and processed and learn the different spices and its significance to the local cuisine. You can also purchase your requirements of spices.
Kandy is well renowned for the Temple of the Tooth and the Royal Botanical Gardens. In Kandy, we will first check in to the hotel and unwind by the poolside. Thereafter we will engage in a Cultural Show and later visit the famous Temple of the tooth which is also popularly known as the “Dalada Maligawa”.
Afterwards, you will return back to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 05 - Kandy - Royal Botanical Gardens - Pinnawela - Kandy
 

Travel Time: Approx 30 minutes
After breakfast at the hotel, you will be taken to visit the beautiful Royal Botanical Gardens. Believed to be one of the most beautiful gardens in South Asia; every variety of tropical and temperate tree and flower is cultivated here. Special features are the Orchid House, the fernery, spice garden, and the trees planted by distinguished visitors to the Island.
Once you have taken in the beauty of the gardens, you will visit the Pinnawela Elephant Orphanage. The Elephant Orphanage is a major tourist attraction where you will see herds of abandoned elephants taken care off with tender love and protection. After spending some memorable time with the elephants, you will return to Kandy and indulge in a city tour and visit a popular gem mining factory and a popular marketplace to get the feel of real Sri Lanka.
Thereafter, you will return back to the hotel for dinner and prepare for departure the next morning.

Day 06 - Kandy - Departure

Travel Time: Approx 3 hours
You will leave Kandy after breakfast en route to the airport, soaking up the memories and experiences of the last 4 days in our paradise island. Your memorable journey with Ceylon Roots comes to an end. (Of course, you are welcome to extend your stay, to maybe visit the beaches in Sri Lanka or any other program you may enjoy. Just let us know in advance.)
We bid you farewell at the airport and wish you a safe flight back home, hoping you would come back again and visit the parts of Sri Lanka that you haven't experienced yet.
